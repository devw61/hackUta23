HACKUTA 2023

About the game

In this horror mystery, a murderer roams the streets and you are the detective trying to solve the murder. By visiting each building you will have the chance to find the murderer as you are given puzzles at each building that will eliminate someone from your suspect list. Based on the campus of the University of Texas at Arlington, solve and capture the murderer in this fun-seeking thriller. Will you be able to solve it? Find out now!

Rules:

Let's Play!

-You will be given a list of people
-Each building you visit, you will be given a puzzle and you must solve it
-From each puzzle you will be able to eliminate a suspect
-After solving each puzzle, you will have deduced the murder
